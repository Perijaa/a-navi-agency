About Omiš

Where the Cetina meets the Adriatic.

Tucked between the towering canyon walls and the open sea, Omiš is one of Dalmatia's best-kept secrets — a medieval pirate town turned premium gateway to the Croatian coast. Our family has been on this promenade for more than fifteen seasons.

Every tour starts within a five-minute walk of the old town. No coaches, no commute — just step from the cobblestones onto the boat.

FAQ

Before you book

No payment upfront. Free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure.

Is the Glass Boat suitable for children?

Yes — it's one of the most popular family tours in Omiš. There are no age restrictions, the ride through the Cetina canyon is smooth and calm, and children love watching fish through the glass bottom. Life jackets in all sizes are on board.

Are pets allowed on board?

Absolutely. The Glass Boat through the Cetina canyon is fully pet-friendly — your dog is welcome aboard at no extra charge. We just ask that they stay calm around other guests.

How long does each tour last?

Glass Boat ~35 min through the canyon + Radmanove Mlinice stop. Taxi Boat 30 min each way + 1.5 h break. Rent a Boat 3 hours or full day. Semi Submarine 1 hour. Rafting ~2.5 hours including two swimming stops.

What's included in the price?

Boat tours include the ride and all scheduled stops. Boat rentals include fuel, safety gear and a map of the riviera. Rafting includes a professional guide, helmet, life jacket and waterproof bag. No hidden fees.

Is rafting safe for beginners and young children?

Yes. The Cetina canyon rafting route is graded for beginners and families. Professional guides who know every metre of the gorge are with you the entire way. Children aged 3 and above can join.

Where do the tours depart from?

Most tours leave from our information desk on the Cetina promenade in Omiš — right where the river meets the old town. Rafting departs from Slime village, a short drive upriver. Exact directions arrive with your booking.

How do I book, and can I cancel?

Book via the form below, by phone, or on WhatsApp — we respond within hours. No payment upfront. Free cancellation or rescheduling up to 24 h before departure. During July and August, book at least one day ahead.
